Our Story

More than a restaurant — a Thai dream on the shores of El Nido.
01

It began in Koh Phangan

Nelly, from Serbia, and Apple, from north-eastern Thailand, met on the island of Koh Phangan — and found a shared passion for Thai cuisine and a vision to create something extraordinary of their own.

02

Learned at eleven

Apple started cooking at just eleven, learning her mother's recipes — the kind passed down through generations. That same love and dedication goes into every dish she prepares today: each meal a celebration of Thai culinary heritage.

03

Born in 2017

They turned the dream into Big Bad Thai — a table where traditional Thai flavour meets the natural beauty of El Nido. Every dish is crafted from fresh, local ingredients and authentic Thai spices, nothing watered down.

04

Above Bacuit Bay

From our Rooftop Lounge and second-floor dining, the whole of Bacuit Bay opens up — limestone cliffs, sea breeze, and a sunset over every plate.
